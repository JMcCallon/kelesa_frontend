How it Works

Describe Job

Explain what kind of help you need, how much you are willing to pay, the level of reviews needed, and when the time that the work must get started
Match & Track

Our algorithm will match you with the best local worker who agrees to the job and you can immediately track their progress to your location
Pay & Review

Receive quality work and easily submit a review and pay for the work without needing checks/cash (unless you want)!
  • Four levels of reviews: (1) E-Mail verified; (2) Facebook or LinkedIn verified; (3) Background checked; or (4) Successfully interviewed by Guy For That corporate team or 10+ reviews and average of at least 4*
  • You set the amount that you want to pay. If it's a reasonable price, we'll find someone in the area who is willing to do the work
  • Tip: increasing the rate will increase the quality of your work
  • No money changes hands until the job is complete
  • If necessary, upload photos of your job
  • This process is easy and simple -- it usually takes less than 2 minutes
  • You can complete this with your phone or on your computer
  • Save your settings in your profile for faster service next time
  • Note 9
  • Our algorithm will find the best worker based on location, ratings/reviews, pay rate, and qualifications
  • You can easily call or text message your worker
  • Follow the route and review the worker's estimated time
  • If you choose to pay based on hourly rate, you pay: [The worker's initial estimated travel time] + [the time the worker is actually on site]
  • If the worker's travel time is extended beyond what is initially expected, you incur no extra charge
  • You cannot schedule a second job while the worker is en route unless you cancel the first job
  • Cancel free of charge if your worker is more than 7 minutes later than the initial expected travel time
  • Once the work is complete, click a few simple buttons and hit submit
  • You can choose whether to pay cash, check, or through our system, which links directly to Paypal
  • Both parties must submit a review (which takes only a few seconds) at end of each job to ensure a fair and consistent system
  • Problem? Let us know about it in the description
  • Ultimately, the deal is between both of you, and will affect both of your reputations. Be fair.
